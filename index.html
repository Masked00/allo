About the Department

The department has well-qualified and experienced faculty who are specialists in the areas of network security and cryptography, databases, wireless networks, artificial intelligence, operating systems and programming languages etc. There are a sizable number of senior faculty members with over 10 years of experience in the stream.The research areas in the department include network security, cryptography, wireless sensor networks, data mining and cloud computing. Our department faculty and students have presented and published papers in many national and international conferences.

About Us

ACE Club (Association of Computer Engineers) started its journey way back in 2011. Since then the club has been run by the students of the CSE department and aims to expose their fellow peers to new technologies and encourage them towards collaborating with similar minded people. The club provides opportunities to become technically strengthened by integrating their skills in various fields of Engineering & Technology, which later in life helps to cope with the highly competitive environment. Our flagship event is a National Level Technical Symposium which was first named “Fenestra'' and later in 2013 was renamed “Ubertech”. This is a stage for participants from various colleges across the country to learn, connect and collaborate. Most of the events conducted by the club promote the technical significance and current trends through workshops, seminars, certification drives and knowledge sessions. One of the major achievements of this symposium is building a healthy relationship with the leading firms of technology and innovation. ”So what are you waiting for, come join us to learn, explore and build together!!”
