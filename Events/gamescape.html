VALORANT
We Are #Valorant

Unrated

Tournament Procedure : The tournament will be held online over a single day. The tournament will consist of many rounds depending on the number of participating teams.

Round Format : Each set of teams will play a game of 5v5 standard Valorant mode against each other in different maps. The map will be decided on the spot from the pool of maps given. (2 chosen by the teams and the third chosen by the organizers).
Whichever team loses will be eliminated.

Rules and Regulations :
Team size : 5 players
The software used is Valorant by Riot (current updated version).
It is a 5 player team tournament (no substitutes).
Each team must present 1 representative. He/she is responsible for his/her team and will be responsible for choosing maps and all communication with authorities must be done through the team captain.
The first team to win 13 rounds (in-game) will be declared the victor of the match and will advance.
The initial fixtures are knockouts and only one map is played. The best of 3 is played for the finals.
The map each team will play will be picked through a map vote.
In case of a draw after 24 rounds, it goes to overtime for 2 rounds. Overtime is continued till there is a result. For the start of the overtime, teams will stay on the side which they played the previous half on, during half-time sides will be swapped.
Players are not allowed to use any form of custom game files during the official matches.
Indulging in malpractices like aimbot, wallhack etc leads to the permanent disqualification of the team.
Minor changes can be done in rules which will be informed to all the participants before the tournament.

Maps :
Ascent, Bind, Haven, Icebox, Split, breeze. (Fracture map not included)

GRAND PRIZE : Rs. 2500

VALORANT DEATH MATCH

Deathmatch mode is quite different from traditional Valorant modes where players must take care of the economy, buy equipment, and plant/disarm Spike.

The rules of this new mode are different: 10 players enter the game and fight free-for-all on a random map;

The player who has the most kills when the time runs out wins;

You join the game with a random agent chosen from among those you own;

Players don't have access to the character's skills so they only have to rely on their weapons;

You can buy any gun in the store. No weapon is forbidden. You will also receive heavy armor;

Killing opponents gives you the so-called Healthy Packs that not only replenish your health to the maximum, but also restore your armor and automatically reload the weapon;

Every now and then all the players are revealed on the map so pay attention to the radar in the top left corner of the screen.

Holding angles and play-styles will not be judged, its up to the players decision.
