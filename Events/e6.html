About the Event.

Lights! Camera! Action! Put on your director cloak and show us your world of cinema.
Capture. Edit. Feature the featurette.IT'S SHOWTIME
Show off your directorial skills with this competition "short films", where a contestant needs to submit a piece of short film they have worked on.

Event Type : Group

Theme: Open theme, with a sensible message.

Judging Criteria:

  • Language bilingual: TAMIL AND ENGLISH
  • NO extreme violence or vulgar language permitted.
  • Originality
  • Creativity
  • Plot
  • Pacing & Structure
  • Characters
  • Cinematography
  • Sound Quality
  • Music Score
  • Entertainment Value
  • Dialogue
  • Message Effectiveness
  • Call to Action

    • Deadline: Submission of the short films should be done on or before 6th of september by 4PM .

    Duration of The short film : 6 to 8 mins (minimum).

    File Type : Must be in MP4 format.

    Fill in the Google Form and join the Whatsapp Group through the link given in the Google Form.
    Submissions will be on Google Drive, and should be sent to ubertechshortfilm2021@gmail.com

    *Please note that your submissions will be accepted only if you complete the registration in the Google Form.*
